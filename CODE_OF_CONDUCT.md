# Code of Conduct

Cyber Code Syndicate is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community.

## Be respectful
- Treat others with respect.
- Assume good intent.
- Give constructive feedback.

## No harassment
Harassment or discrimination of any kind is not tolerated.

## Keep it professional
No doxxing, threats, or abusive behavior.

## Reporting
If something feels off, contact the maintainers privately (see SUPPORT.md).

Maintainers may remove content or restrict access to protect the community and the project.
